About Me:



Greetings!

I'm Derek Johnson, and I've traversed a journey that spans from the heart of Kansas City, Missouri, to the vibrant city of Austin, Texas. After spending most of my life in the Show-Me State, I made the move to Austin in 2019, driven by the desire to be closer to family and embrace new opportunities.

My journey in higher education began at Barton County Community College, where I was not only a dedicated student but also a member of the baseball team. Following my passion for business and economics, I continued my academic pursuits at the University of Missouri in Columbia, earning my degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in Economics in 2019.

Professionally, my diverse experiences have shaped me into a versatile and adaptable individual. From excelling in retail sales and B2B sales to navigating the intricacies of inventory management and manufacturing operations, I've honed valuable skills along the way.

Some of my top attributes include critical thinking, problem-solving, and meticulous attention to detail. I bring technical proficiency in tools like Microsoft Office, Python, HTML, and SQL, showcasing my commitment to staying at the forefront of technological advancements. As a quick learner, I thrive in dynamic environments, eagerly taking on challenges and finding innovative solutions.
